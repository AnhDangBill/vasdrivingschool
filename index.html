Frequently Asked Questions

What do I need to bring to my first class?

+

Please bring a valid learner's permit, a notebook, and any necessary payment for the course if you haven't paid already.

How long are the classes?

+

The full course is 36 hours: 30 hours of classroom instruction and 6 hours of behind-the-wheel driving.

Can I use the school car for the skills test at MVA?

+

Yes, we offer the option to use our school car for your MVA skills test, based on availability. Contact us to schedule in advance.

What are the requirements to get a learner's permit in Maryland?

+

To get a learner's permit in Maryland, you must be at least 15 years and 9 months old, pass a vision screening and knowledge test, and provide required identification documents including proof of identity, Social Security number, and Maryland residency.

How many hours of practice driving do I need before getting a license?

+

In Maryland, drivers under 18 must complete at least 60 hours of practice driving (including 10 hours at night) with a qualified supervising driver. This must be documented on an official practice log, and you must hold your learner's permit for at least 9 months.
