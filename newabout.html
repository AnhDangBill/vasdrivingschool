About VAS Driving School

As the first Vietnamese American driving school in this area, welcome to VAS Driving School. We are committed to providing top-quality instruction that combines safety, skill, and confidence on the road. Our driving school caters to all levels of drivers, from beginners eager to obtain their first license to experienced drivers looking to refine their skills. Your journey to driving success starts here!

Meet Our Team

Tho Tran

Owner/Instructor

As the owner and lead instructor, Tho has over 10 years of experience, helping drivers build confidence behind the wheel. Her passion for teaching ensures each student becomes a safe and responsible driver.

Anh Dang

Executive Director

As the Executive Director, Anh oversees the driving school's operations and ensures a smooth, enjoyable experience for all students. Anh's energetic approach brings fun and engagement to every lesson.

Minh Tran

Marketing Manager

Minh is not only the Marketing Manager but also the friendly voice behind the phone, answering all incoming calls. She ensures that every inquiry is handled with care, while also managing the school’s promotional efforts and community outreach.

Community Engagement

VAS Driving School is proud to be an active member of our local community. Recently, we were a key sponsor at the Mid-Autumn Festival, a beloved celebration for many families in our area. We were honored to sponsor the lion dance team, bringing joy and excitement to the festival, and we also had our own table where we connected with the community and shared information about our services.
