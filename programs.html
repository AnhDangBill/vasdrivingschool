Our Programs & Services

At VAS Driving School, we offer a range of programs to help you gain the skills and confidence needed to drive safely. Below are the services we provide:

Full 36-Hour Driving Course

Our comprehensive 36-hour course covers classroom instruction and behind-the-wheel training to prepare you for the driving test.

▼ Learn more

This program is designed for beginners and those who need to refresh their driving skills. It includes 30 hours of classroom instruction and 6 hours of behind-the-wheel training. By the end of the course, you'll feel confident to take the MVA test.

Am I required to take Driver's Ed? Yes – in Maryland, the law requires all first-time drivers to complete an MVA-certified Driver's Education course to obtain a driver's license. This rule applies to all Maryland residents, regardless of age.

Do I need a learner's permit? You do not need a learner's permit to start the class. You can begin the class as early as age 15. Many students find that taking the class first helps them pass the MVA learner's permit test when they apply for a permit at age 15 and 9 months.

How is the course structured? The classroom portion runs for 2 weeks (Monday through Friday, 10 class days total). Each class is 3 hours long plus a break. The driving portion consists of 3 in-car lessons of 2 hours each, which can be scheduled after completing the classroom portion.

What do I need to bring to class? Please bring: identification (learner's permit, birth certificate, or passport), your payment if not paid in advance, and a pen and notebook for taking notes.

What if I miss a class? You can arrange to make up missed days during a later class. However, MVA policy requires that all students attend the first day of class, and students may not miss more than 4 days total.
