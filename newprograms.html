Our Programs & Services

At VAS Driving School, we offer a range of programs to help you gain the skills and confidence needed to drive safely. Below are the services we provide:

Full 36-Hour Driving Course

Our comprehensive 36-hour course covers classroom instruction and behind-the-wheel training to prepare you for the driving test.

▼ Learn more

This program is designed for beginners and those who need to refresh their driving skills. It includes 30 hours of classroom instruction and 6 hours of behind-the-wheel training. By the end of the course, you’ll feel confident to take the MVA test.

Sign Up

3-Hour Drug & Alcohol Class

This mandatory course teaches the impact of drugs and alcohol on driving and is required for new drivers in Maryland.

▼ Learn more

This 3-hour class is essential for anyone obtaining a driver's license for the first time in Maryland. It covers how drugs and alcohol impair your ability to drive, Maryland’s laws on impaired driving, and the consequences of driving under the influence.

Sign Up

Driver Practice

Perfect for drivers needing extra practice. Get more time behind the wheel with a certified instructor.

▼ Learn more

If you need additional time practicing on the road, our driver practice sessions are perfect. Each session is tailored to your specific needs, helping you improve your driving skills and build confidence on the road.

Sign Up

Car Rental for MVA Test

We offer reliable, fully-insured vehicles for your driving test at the MVA.

▼ Learn more

Need a car for your driving test? We provide safe and reliable vehicles that meet MVA requirements. Rent a car from us for your test and get peace of mind knowing you're driving a vehicle that is in excellent condition and insured for your exam.
